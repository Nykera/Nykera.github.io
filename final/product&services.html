Welcome to Emporium Pies!

Our delicious pies are made fresh to enjoy every day and stuffed to the crust with all of our love.

We hope you enjoy a quick bite of our pies!

  • The Drunken Nut - Bourbon pecan with shortbread crust.
  • Smooth Operator - French silk chocolate with a pretzel crust.
  • Lord of the Pies - Deep dish apple pie with cinnamon streusel.
  • Blue Steel - Flaky all-butter pie crust with a fresh blueberry filling. Notes of lemon, cinnamon and vanilla.
  • Dr Love - Our red velvet chess pie.
  • In The Limelight - Tart, sweet key-lime custard with cream cheese whipped topping.
  • Cloud Nine - Creme pie with salted caramel, butterscotch custard and brown sugar meringue.

We are located at the center of Deep Ellum, offering a panoramic view of Downtown Dallas.
